Slow Cooker Buffalo Chicken Sandwiches

Ingredients

  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
  • 1 (17.5 fluid ounce) bottle of Buffalo Wing Sauce, divided
  • 1/2 (1 ounce) package dry ranch salad dressing mix
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 6 hoagie rolls, split lengthwise

Steps

  1. Place chicken breasts into a slow cooker; pour in 3/4 of the wing sauce and ranch dressing mix.
  2. Cover and cook on Low for 6 to 7 hours.
  3. Shred chicken in the cooker with two forks and stir in the butter.
  4. Pile shredded chicken into hoagie rolls and top with remaining Buffalo Sauce.
  5. Serve and enjoy!
