Mayo Chicken

Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken, cut into 6 pieces
  • 1 cup mayonnaise
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 tablespoon finely chopped fresh rosemary
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • freshly ground black pepper to taste
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Place chicken pieces into a 9x13-inch baking dish.
  3. Stir together mayonnaise, garlic, rosemary, salt, and black pepper in a bowl.
  4. Spread mayonnaise mixture over chicken; top with Parmesan cheese.
  5. Bake until no longer pink in the center and the juices run clear, about 1 hour and 10 minutes.
